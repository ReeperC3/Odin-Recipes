Spaghetti Bolognese



Description

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Mince
  • Carrots
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Fill a pot halfway full with cold water, then put in your spaghetti and put on the grill to boil for 12 minutes.
  2. Put your bolognese sauce, mince, and tomatoes into a pan. You can also grate your carrots and place them in as well. Leave this cooking for 15 and stir it all throughout.
  3. When your spaghetti and mince is finished, place them on your plates to serve. If you would like to add grated cheese on the top too, go ahead.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
